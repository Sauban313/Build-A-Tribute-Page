Free Palestine

Standing for justice and freedom

Here's a brief overview of the Palestinian struggle:


  • Palestine - Palestine is a region in Western Asia that includes the West Bank and Gaza Strip. It has been the center of conflict between Israelis and Palestinians for decades.

  • Occupation - The Palestinian territories have been under Israeli occupation since 1967, leading to widespread human rights abuses and restrictions on movement and access to resources.

  • Settlements - Israel has built illegal settlements in the occupied territories, displacing Palestinian communities and violating international law.

  • Blockade - The Gaza Strip has been under a severe blockade for years, limiting access to basic necessities and hindering economic development.

  • International Support - The Palestinian cause has garnered widespread international support, with calls for an end to the occupation and the establishment of a Palestinian state.

  • Resistance - Palestinians have engaged in various forms of resistance, including protests, civil disobedience, and armed struggle, to demand their rights and freedom.
